ShareRide & Cargo

A unified solution for ride sharing and cargo transportation. Whether you are travelling alone, carrying extra luggage, or urgently need transport between cities — Hayati connects people efficiently, safely, and economically.

Why ShareRide & Cargo?

Traditional transport services operate on fixed schedules. ShareRide & Cargo introduces flexibility, affordability, and availability — even in emergencies.

Cost Efficient

Share fuel expenses, reduce travel costs, and utilize unused vehicle capacity.

Flexible Timing

Travel any time without waiting for fixed bus, train, or airline schedules.

Emergency Friendly

Find available rides instantly during medical, family, or urgent situations.

Cargo Support

Transport luggage, hostel items, or family goods across cities safely.

  • Vehicle details for providers
  • Emergency contact for seekers
  • Travel history tracking
